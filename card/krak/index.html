Krak Card

Issuer: Krak (by Kraken)  |  Network: Mastercard  |  Type: Debit

Kraken's answer to neobanks like Revolut. The Krak Card lets you spend from 400+ crypto and fiat assets anywhere Mastercard is accepted, with real-time conversion at checkout — no manual swaps needed. Zero transaction fees, no FX markup, and instant cashback in fiat or Bitcoin. Currently UK and EU only.
