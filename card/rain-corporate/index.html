Rain Corporate Card

Issuer: Rain  |  Network: Visa  |  Type: Corporate

Rain powers the card programs behind other major crypto companies and offers corporate cards directly to crypto native teams. If your company, DAO, or startup holds its treasury in stablecoins, Rain connects directly to your self-custody wallet, converts to fiat at checkout, and puts it on a Visa that works in over 150 countries. There is no manual off-ramping and no bank account needed. With over 200 partners and $3B+ in annualized volume, Rain offers real-time tracking and multi-chain support.

Pros

  • Auto-converts stablecoins to fiat at checkout
  • Works in 150+ countries on Visa
  • Track every transaction
  • Supports Base, Solana, Polygon, and more chains
  • No off-ramping (no need to manually convert crypto to cash before spending)

Cons

  • Subscription pricing is not public
  • Stablecoins only (USDC, USDT, etc). If you hold ETH, SOL, or other tokens you'll need to swap first

Key Details

  • Cashback: Custom
  • Fee: Subscription
  • Regions: 150+ countries
  • Custody: Self-custody compatible
  • Stablecoins: USDC
