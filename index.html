every crypto card runs on stablecoins under the hood. here's what you're actually holding and spending.

USD 🔵 USDC
USD Coin
by Circle
The safe default. Backed 1:1 by cash and US treasuries, audited monthly. If a card says "spend stablecoins" — it probably means this one.
Tuyo MetaMask Coinbase Bleap Gnosis Pay
USD 🟢 USDT
Tether
by Tether Limited
The most liquid stablecoin on earth. Bigger market cap than USDC, accepted everywhere, especially in Asia and LATAM. More controversial on reserve transparency.
KAST 1inch Bybit Crypto.com
USD 🦊 mUSD
MetaMask USD
by Stripe (Bridge)
Brand new. This is what MetaMask pays you cashback in. Built on Ethereum, issued through Stripe's Bridge infrastructure. Tied to the MetaMask rewards ecosystem.
MetaMask
USD yield-bearing 🏦 aUSDC
Aave USDC
by Aave Protocol
USDC deposited into Aave that earns yield automatically. Hold this instead of regular USDC and your balance grows while you wait to spend. DeFi savings account energy.
MetaMask ether.fi
EUR / GBP 🇪🇺 EURe / GBPe
Monerium Euro & Pound
by Monerium
On-chain euro and pound. If you're spending in Europe, your card converts to these. Regulated as e-money under EU law — as legit as stablecoins get.
Gnosis Pay
USD ⚗️ DAI
Dai
by Sky (MakerDAO)
The OG decentralized stablecoin. Not backed by a company — maintained by smart contracts and crypto collateral. For the "don't trust, verify" crowd.
